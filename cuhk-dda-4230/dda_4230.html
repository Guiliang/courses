DDA-4230: Reinforcement Learning

Course Introduction

This course provides a basic introduction to reinforcement learning algorithms and their applications. Topics include:

  • Multi-armed bandits; finite Markov decision processes; dynamic programming; Monte-Carlo methods; temporal-difference learning; actor-critic methods; off-policy learning.
  • Introduction to deep variants of the aforementioned algorithms, including deep Q-learning, policy gradient methods, and actor-critic methods.
